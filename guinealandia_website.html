About Guinealandia

Guinealandia is a fictional sovereign empire inspired by guinea pigs. It symbolizes peace, cuteness, unity, and royal fluffiness.

History

Guinealandia was founded as Velká Morčská Říše and later renamed to Morčlandia, internationally known as Guinealandia. The empire is ruled by wisdom, hay, and absolute squeaking authority.

National Flag

The flag uses colors inspired by guinea pigs:

  • Orange – fur and warmth
  • White – purity and peace
  • Brown – nature and hay fields

A golden crown represents royal guinea pig sovereignty.

Culture

The culture of Guinealandia revolves around community, food, naps, and ceremonial squeaks. National holidays include Hay Day and The Great Feeding.
